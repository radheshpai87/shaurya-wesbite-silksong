Hollow Knight: Silksong

A New Journey Begins in the Kingdom of Pharloom

About the Game

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the highly anticipated sequel to the award-winning indie game Hollow Knight, developed by Team Cherry. You play as Hornet, princess-protector of Hallownest, who finds herself in a new kingdom called Pharloom. Battle foes, explore vast landscapes, and uncover mysteries in this beautifully hand-drawn Metroidvania adventure.

Game Highlights

  • Play as Hornet, a fast and agile new protagonist.
  • Explore the mysterious kingdom of Pharloom.
  • Encounter over 200 unique enemies and legendary bosses.
  • Craft tools and use powerful “Silk Skills.”
  • Discover quests and hidden secrets throughout the world.
